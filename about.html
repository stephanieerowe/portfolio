Background

Who I am.

I'm a builder who moves fluidly across the entire tech stack. I started with web development, moved into systems thinking and backend architecture, and have spent recent years deep in AI—exploring LLMs, machine learning pipelines, and intelligent automation.

I'm genuinely excited by problems that require cross-domain thinking: building systems that are both technically sound and user-focused, or using AI to solve real problems in creative ways.

What I value

  • Solving real problems, not just following trends.
  • Code that is readable, testable, and maintainable.
  • Understanding the "why" before jumping to implementation.
  • Systems that are both powerful and pragmatic.
